Why is snow removal important?

Snow removal and having a clean home go hand in hand.

Tracking snow into your home is important to avoid for a number of reasons. The unwanted germs and bacteria snow absorbs and spreads are best to keep outside of the home, especially during the winter months when the flu and cold season is upon us. Keeping your carpets bright and new looking is more of a chore when you have extra moisture, dirt, and de-icing chemicals being soaked up. If you have hardwood floors in your home, you know the risks of damaging your floor when bringing in ice, sludge, and snow. Take on the winter chore with Team Clean’s snow removal services and bring more convenience and peace of mind to your busy schedule.

Snow removal services ensure your property is safe during icy months.

Everybody knows somebody who has suffered a serious injury while attempting snow removal. Slipping and falling while walking on icy or snowy paths is an all too real occurrence. Thorough snow removal is the first step to preventing such accidents. Ongoing maintenance helps keep your sidewalks, steps, and driveways safe and dry. The faster snow is removed from pathways, the less likely ice will start to form. Reduce the chances of injury to yourself and others by keeping pathways and driveways clear of snow and ice throughout the entire winter season. Team Clean has earned their expertise by providing snow removal services to households like yours since 2004.

Cracks in concrete and pavement, plants halfway dug out of the ground or destroyed by shovel, build up of de-icing salt… these are a few common outcomes of improper snow removal.
