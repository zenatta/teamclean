How often should you get a carpet cleaning?

Carpet cleaning should be scheduled every 6 months, at a minimum. Any longer and you risk the soil being pressed into the underlay and causing a buildup of bacteria and allergens in your carpet. Professional carpet cleaning services are generally required more frequently if your carpets see more traffic or you often experience snowy, rainy or muddy days.

Carpet cleaning reduces indoor air pollution.

According to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), dirty carpets absorb and then release a plethora of indoor air pollutants, including toxic airborne gases that easily get trapped in your carpet. Regular professional carpet cleaning can help remove these issues and improve the quality of the air you breathe.

Carpet cleaning reduces allergens and prevents mold.

Your carpet behaves similarly to the filter in your heating / air conditioning system. Airborne particles like mold spores, allergens and other unwanted bacteria get trapped in carpets, then overtime, released back into the air. Regular carpet cleaning prevents the buildup of allergens and inhibits the growth of mold and mildew.

What are the best approaches to carpet cleaning in Breckenridge?


Invest in quality doormats for every entrance.

Doormats are underrated. Wiping your shoes off before stepping into your home goes a long way in terms of protecting your floors and carpeting. Everybody in Breckenridge knows exactly how messy snow, salt, and slush can be during the cold weather seasons. Putting down a doormat for you and your guests to wipe your shoes off on as you come is always a good idea. If you want to take it a step further, consider putting down a doormat outside of your door to wipe your shoes on and another inside to keep your floors clean as you take off your shoes.


Always take your shoes off when you come inside.

If you are serious about carpet cleaning in Breckenridge, make a rule that shoes must come off when you walk in so you don’t bring in dirt and grime from outside your home. Having designated house shoes or slippers to wear around your home will help maintain clean flooring, and they keep your feet warm!


If you can splurge on a high quality vacuum, do it.

Regularly vacuuming your carpets is one of, if not THE, most important aspects of carpet cleaning Breckenridge and can extend the lifespan of your carpets. You may be tempted to save a little money and get an inexpensive vacuum to handle the job. If you have a lot of carpet cleaning to take care of or many area rugs in your home, then this is not a wise idea. The adage “You get what you pay for.” definitely applies in the world of vacuum cleaners.


Speaking of vacuuming, do it twice a week.

Vacuuming the house twice a week should be your minimum. If you have kids or pets or tend to have frequent guests, plan to vacuum accordingly. Martha Stewart, expert of all matters related to housekeeping, advises to use a certain technique to get the most out of your efforts. Go over each spot on your carpet 8 to 12 times, depending on the level of traffic in that area. Also, deliberately vacuum in all directions to get the most out of your carpet cleaning efforts. If you don’t think you can fit multiple vacuuming sessions in every week, consider getting an automatic robot vacuum to supplement thorough vacuuming sessions. Carpet cleaning in Breckenridge takes time.


Never give a stain time to set.

The more time that passes between spills, food, or pet stains and when you start cleaning the messes, the harder it is to prevent a stain. You should always be diligent about cleaning up any messes immediately. Also, do not treat every mess equally. Some materials and types of stains require different techniques and methods to properly clean. The wrong solution can potentially be more problematic than the original mess, so pay attention to care instructions.


Keep your carpet fresh!

Beyond vacuuming and cleaning up stains, there are a variety of ways you can freshen up your carpets. Borax, hydrogen peroxide, vinegar, rubbing alcohol, baking soda, peppermint, rosemary, and cinnamon are all common household items that can help you keep your carpet clean, fresh, and extend its lifetime!


Schedule professional maintenance at least once a year.

To keep your carpets clean and in tip-top shape for as long as possible, you need to invest in professional cleaning. The bare minimum would be once a year, but if you live in an area that experiences snow and icy weather, like Colorado, you might need a professional to provide carpet cleaning services in Breckenridge on a more frequent basis.
