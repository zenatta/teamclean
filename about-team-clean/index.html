About

"The success of any company depends on the quality and integrity of its team."

Who is Team Clean?

Team Clean was established in Dillon, Colorado, in 2004. The name Team Clean originates from the core belief that the success of any company depends on the quality and integrity of its team. To help foster these values, we provide technical training to each of our employees, and in addition to the training, we offer employees excellent benefits like retirement, paid vacation, and holiday bonuses. Team Clean would be nothing without its team, and we are proud to have cultivated such a wholesome culture in our company. It is our firm belief that establishing this culture has been the foundation to Team Clean’s success as a trusted service provider. We aim to contribute to the wellbeing of our employees and clients for many future years.

Our cleaning services are the best.

We have consistently been voted as the best cleaning company and best janitorial service in local news publications. Our exceptional concierge cleaning service has established and maintained a reputation for creating cleaner, healthier environments everywhere we go. Every member of Team Clean loves what they do and is grateful to each person, household, and business we have served since 2004. We look forward to many more years of teaming up to reduce the germs and dust in your home and increase the time you have to enjoy doing what you love most.

Team Clean is following all CDC guidelines and recommendations to stay clean and healthy during COVID-19.
