Residential building maintenance you can rely on.

Apartment buildings, condos, and HOAs can benefit from the building maintenance services Team Clean provides. We will professionally clean and restock all of your communal spaces and offices. Some of the duties provided by our building maintenance services include cleaning and restocking restrooms, kitchens and break rooms, floor maintenance, dusting, as well as glass and mirror cleaning. Team Clean specializes in caring for residential buildings and would love to help keep your property clean and healthy for years to come.

Reduce germs and reduce sick days.

It has been reported that regular cleaning significantly reduces the amount of microorganisms and unwanted bacteria found on surfaces and in the air. Maintaining a clean environment helps to ensure your property stays as healthy as possible. Team Clean’s building maintenance services are fully compliant with all OSHA standards and have been keeping local residential buildings clean and healthy for decades. We have the expertise required to properly clean and maintain your residential building.
