A clean workplace is a productive workplace.

Providing the cleanest, healthiest environment to your employees is an important part of running a successful business. When you consider the amount of work a sick employee misses and how long it takes them to get brought back up to speed, the cost of an infectious disease outbreak in the workplace can be astronomical. This cost far outweighs the cost of hiring an experienced commercial cleaning service. The professionals at Team Clean will put into place cleaning techniques that are proven to reduce the spread of microorganisms and instances of infection.

Outsource the dirty work and save money.

For most companies, hiring an outside commercial cleaning service is less expensive than hiring an in-house team. The International Facilities Managers Association (IFMA) released a report stating that:

  • 52% of facility managers claim savings from outsourcing custodial operations.
  • In-house teams cost an estimated 40 cents more per square foot than outsourced teams.
  • In-house custodial productivity can fall significantly below industry norms.
  • Outsourced custodial companies can provide additional, specialty services that simply aren’t available from in-house resources.
